Data Summary

Despite the ongoing refinement of the human proteome, there are still a lot of missing or unexplored regions in the proteome which has sufficient evidence at the transcript level. This gap in the knowledge of proteome can be filled by studying proteoforms that majorly arise from sequence polymorphism, alternative splicing and proteolysis. Exploring proteoforms will provide insights into the fundamental working of biological systems as well as could explain disease inception.
